Mona Megistus

About

Astrologist Mona Megistus is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact. An astrologist of knowledgeable skill and high pride, Mona has settled in Mondstadt to avoid suffering the ire of her master after unwittingly reading the latter's diary.

Characteristics

  1. She is particularly picky when it comes to eating.
  2. She does not tolerate those who disrespect her.
  3. She also makes a lot of denials when someone is pointing out what she doesn't have.
